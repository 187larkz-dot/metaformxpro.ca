WoodWash

The specialized cleaner to prepare and maintain your panels during WoodShield application.

Optimized cleaning before application

WoodWash effectively eliminates concrete residues, residual oils and all contamination on the wood surface, allowing WoodShield to fully penetrate the fibres.

Prepares the surface for maximum protection

By degreasing and opening the wood pores, WoodWash ensures WoodShield adheres perfectly for optimal and uniform long-lasting protection.

Compatible with all formwork types

Formulated for standard formwork plywood, HDO panels and treated wood. Fast-acting, non-abrasive, respectful of wood fibres.
